Notifications

Examples of notifications.

Info

  • Notifications are meant to provide the user with feedback based on completing an action or the lack of completion.
  • They appear near the top of the page.
  • May have timed visibility (10 seconds) and/or be dismissed by the user by clicking the "x" icon.
  • Should span the width of the viewport.
  • The content should be kept short and concise.
  • If the content wraps to a new line the icon on the left should remain vertically centered, but the close icon should remain in place at the top right.
